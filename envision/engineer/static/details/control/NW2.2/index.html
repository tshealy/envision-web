NW2.2 MANAGE STORMWATER

Intent: Minimize the impact of development on stormwater runoff quantity, rate, and quality.

Metric: Degree to which the project infiltrates, evapotranspirates, reuses, filters, and/or treats stormwater while not exceeding rate or quantity runoff targets.

Levels of Achievement

Improved

(2) Expanded Options

(A) Infiltrate, evapotranspirate, reuse, filter, and/or treat 100% of the 85th percentile 24-hour event. Ensure compliance with local requirements if these are stricter.

(B) Do not exceed rate or quantity of runoff for the 2-year 24-hour rainfall event relative to the pre- existing condition (greenfield, greyfield, or brownfield).

Enhanced

(4) 85th percentile / 2-year event

(A) Infiltrate, evapotranspirate, and/or reuse 100% of 85th percentile 24-hour event. Ensure compliance with local requirements if these are stricter.

OR

Detain and filter 150% of 85th percentile 24-hour event.

(B) Do not exceed rate or quantity of runoff for the 2-year 24-hour rainfall event relative to the pre- existing condition (greenfield, greyfield, or brownfield).

(C) The project includes an erosion, sedimentation, and pollutant control plan for construction activities.

Superior

(9) 90th percentile / 10-year event

(A) Infiltrate, evapotranspirate, and/or reuse 100% of 90th percentile 24-hour event.

OR

Detain and filter 150% of 90th percentile 24-hour event.

(B) Do not exceed rate or quantity of runoff for the 2, 5, and 10-year 24-hour rainfall event relative to the pre-existing condition (greenfield, greyfield, or brownfield).

(C) The project includes an erosion, sedimentation, and pollutant control plan for construction activities.

Conserving

(17) 95th percentile / 50-year event

(A) Infiltrate, evapotranspirate, or reuse 100% of 95th percentile 24-hour event.

OR

Detain and filter 150% of 95th percentile 24-hour event.

(B) Do not exceed rate or quantity of runoff for the 2, 5, 10, 25 and 50- year 24-hour rainfall event relative to the pre-existing condition (greenfield, greyfield, or brownfield).

(C)The project includes an erosion, sedimentation, and pollutant control plan for construction activities.

Restorative

(24) 95th percentile / 100-year event

(A) Infiltrate, evapotranspirate, or reuse more than 100% of 95th percentile 24-hour event.

OR

Detain and filter more than 150% of 95th percentile 24-hour event.

(B) Do not exceed rate or quantity of runoff for the 2, 5, 10, 25, 50 and 100-year 24-hour rainfall event relative to the pre-existing condition (greenfield, greyfield, or brownfield).

(C) The project includes an erosion, sedimentation, and pollutant control plan for construction activities.

(D) The project manages or treats stormwater on or from other sites according to criterion A.

OR

The project demonstrates that it contributes to, or is part of, a larger watershed plan for managing stormwater runoff quantity, rate, and/or quality.

If the project is located in a region where construction erosion and sedimentation are not regulated the plan is demonstrated to comply with industry accepted best practices.
