Information about Being in a Research Study

Sustainable Infrastructure Rating Systems

Clemson University and Virginia Tech

Description of the Study and Your Part in It

We are inviting you to take part in a research study. The purpose of this research is to assess and improve sustainability rating systems. Your part in the study will be to respond to survey questions. It will take you about one hour to participate in this study. Selected participants will have the opportunity to respond to interview questions, which, if you agree to participate, should about 20 minutes.

Risks and Discomforts

We do not know of any risks or discomforts to you in this research study.

Possible Benefits

We expect that you will learn about sustainable infrastructure and the Envision rating system by taking part in this study.

Incentives

None.

Protection of Privacy and Confidentiality

We will do everything we can to protect your privacy and confidentiality. We will not ask you any personally identifying information.

Choosing to Be in the Study

You do not have to be in this study. You may choose not to take part and you may choose to stop taking part at any time. You will not be punished in any way if you decide not to be in the study or to stop taking part in the study.
