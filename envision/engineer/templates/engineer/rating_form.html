Instructions Before You Get Started!

Each of the credits contains a set of evaluation criteria, listed in the "details/guidance" link, that assessors will look for when determining if the qualifications for each level of achievement have been met. This is essentially a step- by-step outline of how to meet the credit requirements. The number of requirements increases with each level of achievement. To mimic this for the training, a text box for each credit is provide for you to explain how you plan to meet the level of achievement you selected. As you move up in achievement, your explanation of how you plan to meet these requirements also increases. If you decide a credit is not applicable you must also provide an explanation.

An example project: South LA Wetlands

Here is an example project to help with your assessment. The South Los Angeles Wetland Park is a nature park and sustainable stormwater treatment wetland constructed on a former Brownfield site in Los Angeles, CA. Both the previous site and end result are similar to what is possible in Cordova, AL. Included below are examples to explain how the South LA team achieved a platinum rating.
