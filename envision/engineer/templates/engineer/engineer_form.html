Welcome to the Envision Case Study Training Program!

The purpose of this training is to provide real-world examples of how The Envision Rating System for Sustainable Infrastructure is used to design and assess infrastructure projects. Below is a case study about Cordova, AL. To begin the training read through the documentation.

Cordova, AL

The city of Cordova, AL hired your firm to transform a dilapidated, under-used park and brownfield site into a community walking and outdoor gathering center. Your job is to ensure the design meets Envision certification. Because the project is in the early design stages, you are in charge of many project decisions. For example, is reducing light pollution of value? What about community walkability?

Park and Brownfield Redevelopment

The site includes a floodplain and wetlands as part of the stream that needs repairing. You can add protective buffer zones to enhance and restore the waterbody.

Exact details about what to include in the park (e.g. stream restoration, education facility, wildlife habitat, nature trails, bike paths) are up to you.
